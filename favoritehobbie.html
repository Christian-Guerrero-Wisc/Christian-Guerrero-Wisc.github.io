I have been doing martial arts since my Junior year of high school. I started with Kickboxing and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) at a local gym in Saipan. I have been doing martial arts for almost 4 years now and I will continue to do it for as long as I can. I have made many friends through martial arts and I have learned a lot about myself and others. I started martial arts because I could not find a ball or team sport that I enjoyed or have been good at. I never really had playing time in those high school teams since I was not as good as others so I got bored easily, but with martial arts, I am never bored and always getting action. I recommend anyone with any athletic ability or skill to get into it because it teaches you endurance, tenacity, and how to push yourself through pain and discomfort.
