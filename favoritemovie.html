Movie Information

  • Director: George Lucas

From the best of my memory, this is the first movie I remember ever watching. I was five years old and my mom was recommended by her friends to let me watch the movie since they were Sci-Fi fans themselves. Since my first viewing, I have probably watched it over a hundred times. It made me a huge star wars nerds and while I may not watch a lot of Star Wars today besides the original movies, I read a lot of Star Wars graphic novels and comics and have Star Wars themed itmes like blankets and phone cases.
