Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Egg yolks
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Bacon (or pancetta/guanciale)
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic (optional)

While pasta as a category of food is not my favorite, my favorite food of all time is this one particular pasta dish. This dish is called Carbonara. It is an easy dish to make and variations can be made (to most Italians' disliking) to fit however you want it to turn out for yourself. Me personally, I like to keep it traditional as possible with the exception of using good old classic breakfast bacon instead of pancetta or guanciale. I also like to add a little bit of garlic into it as well. I learned this recipe from one of my favorite cooking Youtubers, Binging with Babish, whose video I learned how to cook the dish is linked with the image above.
